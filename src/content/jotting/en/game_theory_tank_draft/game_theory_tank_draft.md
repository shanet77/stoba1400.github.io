I'm a big fan of the [Game Theory podcast](https://www.youtube.com/c/gametheorypodcastwsamvecenie) hosted by Sam Vecenie and Bryce Simon. They do a great job explaining basketball for all levels of fans of the game. They recently did a "Tank Commanders Sicko Draft" ([episode link](https://www.youtube.com/live/iYK5v8upsek?si=zrI5NBsEjid4O9-n)).

I __love__ March/April basketball. The Corey Brewer 50-ball, Terrence Ross, that one time [Taurean Prince went toe-to-toe with Giannis](https://youtu.be/CwF9F6tdIF4?si=SytwLvBi_rlrNoID)[^1]

With all the chaos of the season, and not to speak of the world itself, it would be nice to track the points for these players incase the topic gets lost.
